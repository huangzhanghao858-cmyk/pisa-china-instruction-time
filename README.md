# pisa-china-instruction-time

Replication and extension of Lavy (2015) and Bietenbeck & Collins (2023) using Chinese PISA 2009–2018 data. Examines the "zero effect" of instruction time on academic achievement in China, with subject heterogeneity and nonlinear analyses.

## Data

### Chinese PISA Sample (`china_all.dta`)
Constructed from raw PISA student questionnaire data for Chinese participating regions:
- 2009, 2012: Shanghai
- 2015: Beijing, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Guangdong
- 2018: Beijing, Shanghai, Jiangsu, Zhejiang

Raw data available at: https://www.oecd.org/pisa/data/

### OECD Cross-Country Sample (`pisa_stack.csv`)
Public dataset provided by Bietenbeck & Collins (2023), covering 22 OECD countries across 6 PISA rounds (2000–2018).

Download from: https://doi.org/10.1002/jae.2957 (replication files)

## References
- Lavy V. (2015). The Economic Journal, 125(588): F397–F424.
- Bietenbeck J, Collins M. (2023). Journal of Applied Econometrics, 38(3): 423–431.
